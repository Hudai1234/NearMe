Thangam sweets

Thangam Sweets" can refer to several different businesses, but the name is associated with shops in Tamil Nadu like one in Dindigul known for natural, preservative-free sweets and another in Coimbatore that offers delivery. The name itself is the Tamil word for "gold". Many of these establishments prioritize quality ingredients and traditional methods to produce authentic-tasting sweets and savories for events and everyday consumption.
